Mary Sue Neumann

a woman who lived and loved.

Shown above with her husband Charles J. Neumann

Here is a timeline of Sue's life:

  • 1936-born in Calumet, Oklahoma on the 2nd of January.
  • 1958-married on the 23rd of August to Charles Neumann.
  • 1961-gave birth to daughter Cim Sue Neumann.
  • 1970's-national training director for Gloria Marshall's Figure Salon.
  • 1980's-Legal Secretary for US Air Force.
  • 2016-Passed away unexpectedly on the 22nd of July.
